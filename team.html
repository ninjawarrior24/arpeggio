Connections: Trustworthy companies that we do business with

BeONE Sports

Partner

BeONE Sports is an innovative sports technology company that is democratizing elite athletic training through a mobile-first, AI-driven platform. Their core technology uses computer vision and machine learning to analyze an athlete's biomechanics using nothing more than a standard smartphone or mobile device—eliminating the need for expensive wearables or motion capture labs. This enables "Comparative Training," where users can record themselves and receive instant, data-driven feedback by comparing their form and technique against a vast, proprietary catalog of movements from collegiate, professional, and Olympic athletes. The company's mission is to flatten the learning curve for athletes of all levels by providing affordable, accessible, and personalized coaching insights to maximize potential and minimize injury risk.

Nexrage Studios

Creative Studio

Nexrage Studios is a Dallas-based venture studio specializing in custom software development, immersive experiences, and game development. They deliver secure, compliant technology solutions for startups and enterprise clients across high-growth industries including Healthcare, FinTech, and Gaming. Their expertise includes Artificial Intelligence & Machine Learning, Mobile & Web App Development, Augmented Reality (AR) & Virtual Reality (VR) experiences, and Game Development for PC, console, and mobile. Nexrage focuses on providing a closed-loop system that handles the entire process from discovery to deployment, transforming businesses with scalable, high-quality, and secure technology solutions.

Belltower Technologies

Technology Partner

Belltower Technologies is a critical food safety and incident management company that operates the Instant Recall™ system. Instant Recall™ is a high-speed, multi-channel communication platform (phone, text, email) designed to ensure instant notification and acknowledgement for product recalls across massive supply chains. Used by large distributors, the service guarantees timely contact to all affected locations, provides recall details, and manages the collection of inventory reporting, helping businesses mitigate risk, protect public health, and maintain regulatory compliance during a crisis.

South Star Systems

Hardware & Systems

SouthStar Systems is a lighting company that offers turn-key LED lighting solutions for commercial, industrial, and sports facilities. Their services include lighting assessments, custom design, and project management to deliver tailored solutions for new construction or retrofit projects. A notable product is their line of portable, battery-powered Sports-LITE Training Lights, which allow sports areas to be illuminated without a generator. They also provide rugged, high-powered LED lighting towers and LED display solutions. SouthStar focuses on high-efficiency LED technology and optimized illumination to reduce energy consumption and minimize light pollution.
